Status Saver – Video Saver – Privacy Policy

WhatsApp Status Saver (Images & Videos)

Last updated: 22 Feb 2026 Contact: ssnapstatus@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy explains how Status Saver – Video Saver ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Status Saver – Video Saver mobile application (the "App").

Status Saver – Video Saver is designed to help you view and save media that is already stored on your device (such as WhatsApp status images/videos) and to optionally save copies into your device gallery.

1) Information We Collect

Media access (on your device)

The App accesses media files on your device to show WhatsApp status content and let you save it. We do not upload your photos/videos to our servers.

App data (local only)

The App stores some information locally on your device (for example: viewed status state, your preferences, and saved items index) to provide a better experience.

What we do NOT collect

  • We do not ask for your WhatsApp messages, contacts, or account password.
  • We do not collect your personal photos/videos for uploading to a server.
  • We do not sell your personal data.

2) Permissions & Why We Need Them

Status Saver – Video Saver requests limited permissions only to provide core features. The exact permissions depend on your Android version.

Media permissions

  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES (Android 13+): to read status images on your device.
  • READ_MEDIA_VIDEO (Android 13+): to read status videos on your device.
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 12 and below): to read status media stored on your device.

Without these permissions, the App cannot display WhatsApp statuses.

Notifications permission

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS (Android 13+): to show notifications when new statuses are detected (if you enable notifications).

Notifications are optional. You can disable them anytime from the App Settings.

3) How We Use Information

  • To display available WhatsApp status images/videos on your device.
  • To save selected statuses to your gallery or to a private in-app vault (when you use the save feature).
  • To show notifications about new statuses (only if enabled).
  • To improve app stability and performance.

4) Ads

Status Saver – Video Saver may display advertisements (for example, Google AdMob). Ad providers may collect certain information (such as advertising identifiers) to serve relevant ads and measure performance.

To learn more about how Google uses data, you can review Google's policy: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

5) Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share limited information only in these cases:

  • With service providers (e.g., ad services) as needed to deliver their functionality.
  • For legal reasons if required by law.

6) Data Storage & Security

  • Status content is read from your device and saved to your device only when you choose to save.
  • We store basic preferences and state locally on your device.
  • We take reasonable measures to protect the information we handle, but no method is 100% secure.

7) Children's Privacy

Status Saver – Video Saver is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8) Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
